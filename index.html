sustainability practices

Here at Habitat, it is our mission to provide a comfortable stay without having to sacrifice quality for more eco-conscious option. Run completely off of renewable energy resources, Habitat is the leading place for earth minded travelers. We support local farmers and businesses to cut down on shipping emissions, and every product we deem as ‘single use’ is recyclable or compostable. We get it—traveling is a huge part of today’s world, but we are trying to keep this world worth traveling.
-Habitat
